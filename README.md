# How to Play this Manual
This manual is intended to play on a freshly created character. You may play on a server or by yourself, there is no difference in logic!

These things are randomized; Blood Types, Weapons, Spells, Spell Jewel Tiers, Passives, Items received from Knowledge Stations and Bosses.

The logic is based around Normal Difficulty, I do not recommend it for Brutal!

When you obtain an item from the Multiworld, you are then allowed to use said item. 

Any other items and structures you obtain from defeating a boss may be used freely!

## Different Goals in this Manual
Currently there are four different goals in this manual.
- Act 1 Goal - Requires you to defeat Quincy the Bandit King
- Act 2 Goal - Requires you to defeat Octavian the Militia Captain
- Act 3 Goal - Requires you to defeat Cyril the Cursed Smith
- Dracula Goal - Defeat Dracula and complete the Journal
- Defeat them All - Requires you to defeat all of the V-Blood bosses!
While you may pick lower Act goals, locations are not reduced if you do so. This is because the items that are randomized are too many and would break generation.

## Optional Yaml Options
- Progressive Bosses - When this option is enabled, you will have to defeat every Boss in its row before tackling the next row of bosses.
- Fishsanity - When this option is enabled there will be a location added for each fish that exist in the game. Will also add the various fishes as filler items if you do.
- Spell School Mastery - When this option is enabled, it will add Spell Mastery items into your item pool for each Spell School. You are not allowed to learn any of that specific spell school of spells until you receive them.
